### Full Description

UwU Weather is a lightweight, beautifully designed weather app that lets you check the forecast in seconds.

Features:

- Instant current conditions including temperature, humidity, and more
- Multiple themes to match your style (glassmorphism UI)
- Quick city shortcuts for London, Paris, Tokyo, and more
- Fast and responsive, works great offline with cached data
- Clean, minimal design built for everyday use

No bloat, no clutter. Just the weather you need, when you need it.

Built with love by UwU Apps.
